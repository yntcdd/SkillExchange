Squeaky

Squeaky is an energetic little hamster who loves climbing, digging, and running on her wheel. Her favorite snacks are pumpkin seeds and pieces of cucumber, which keep her hydrated and active. She’s sensitive to cedar bedding, so her home is made with hypoallergenic materials. Squeaky’s lively personality makes her a delightful pet for those who enjoy watching their pet’s antics and providing an enriching environment.
