Rocky

Rocky is a sweet and sociable guinea pig who loves munching on fresh lettuce and the occasional bell pepper slice. Rocky is sensitive to strong scents, so him environment is kept fragrance-free to avoid any allergic reactions. His gentle, calm nature makes him a great pet for families who want a small, loving companion.
