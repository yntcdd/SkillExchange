Whiskers

Whiskers is a gentle Maine Coon who enjoys lounging by windows and watching birds outside. He loves being brushed and enjoys the occasional play session with feather toys. His favorite treats are small pieces of tuna, which he pairs with fresh water. Whiskers is sensitive to dairy, so his treats are always non-dairy based. His calm and affectionate personality makes Whiskers an ideal pet for someone who enjoys quiet companionship.
