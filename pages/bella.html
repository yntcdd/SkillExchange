Bella

Bella is a sweet, loving Poodle with a playful personality. She loves nothing more than her meal times, often trotting eagerly to her bowl at feeding time. Her favorite snacks are small pieces of apple and chicken bites, paired with fresh, cool water. Bella is particularly fond of obedience training and loves learning new tricks, which she performs with flair. She’s allergic to dairy, so her treats are always lactose-free. Bella’s intelligence and eagerness to please make her a wonderful pet for families who enjoy teaching and interacting with their pets.
