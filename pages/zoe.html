Zoe

Zoe is a curious hamster with a big personality packed into a tiny body. She loves exploring her tunnels, running on her wheel, and hiding treats in her little nooks. Zoe’s favorite snack is sunflower seeds, which she savors alongside a drop of fresh water. She’s very particular about her bedding and enjoys keeping a clean environment. Zoe is sensitive to scented products, as she’s allergic to artificial fragrances, so her surroundings are always kept natural. This little hamster is a bundle of joy for anyone looking for a pet who’s active, curious, and endlessly entertaining.
