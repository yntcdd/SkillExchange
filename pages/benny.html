Benny

Benny is a high-spirited Shiba Inu who loves spending his afternoons outdoors, whether it's hiking trails, running across open fields, or splashing in shallow rivers. His favorite treat is a crunchy carrot, paired with a little chicken broth, which keeps him fueled for his adventures. Benny's loyal and intelligent nature makes him an ideal companion for active families. He's quite cautious around bees, as he's allergic to stings, and sensitive to wheat, so he sticks to a grain-free diet. After a day out, Benny enjoys curling up by his human's side for a good rest.
