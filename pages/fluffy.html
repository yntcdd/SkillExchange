Fluffy

Fluffy is a soft, fluffy rabbit with a calm and curious demeanor. She loves nibbling on fresh vegetables, especially carrots, and enjoys chewing on safe wooden toys. Her favorite drink is water infused with a bit of mint, which keeps her cool. Fluffy is sensitive to dusty bedding, so her environment is always kept clean. This rabbit is a perfect choice for a family looking for a gentle, low-maintenance pet with a big heart.
