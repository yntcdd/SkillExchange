Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the Pet Adoption Matchmaking Portal?

Our portal helps connect potential pet owners with their perfect furry (or feathered!) companion using advanced matchmaking tools.

2. How do I adopt a pet?

Simply browse our available pets, take the compatibility quiz, and contact the shelter or owner directly through the pet’s profile page.

3. What types of pets are available for adoption?

We feature a wide range of pets, including dogs, cats, rabbits, birds, hamsters, and more!

4. Is there a fee to adopt a pet?

Adoption fees vary by shelter and the type of pet. All fees are listed on the pet’s profile page.

5. Can I return a pet if it doesn’t work out?

Each shelter has its own policy regarding returns. Please check the shelter’s terms before adopting.

6. How do I know if a pet is family-friendly?

Our pet profiles indicate whether a pet is suitable for families with children. You can also filter pets using our quiz.

7. How can I contact you for more help?

Visit our Contact Us page to send us a message or view our support options.
